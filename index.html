Prediction Analysis

Upload prediction files from AlphaFold3, ColabFold, Boltz, Chai-1, or OpenFold. Calculate iLIS metrics and view PAE/LIS/cLIS maps.

e.g., Upload ColabFold .zip or AlphaFold3 output

FlyPredictome

Search Drosophila gene interactions from FlyPredictome. Calculate iLIS metrics and view contact maps.

e.g., Akt1–foxo, Dl–N, Dsh–Axn

Human Kinase-TF Interactome

Search human kinase-transcription factor interactions from FlyPredictome ortholog predictions (Kim et al. 2025).

e.g., TP53, CDK2, AKT1, BRAF, EGFR

AlphaFold DB Dimer

Search AlphaFold Protein Structure Database for dimer predictions by UniProt ID. Calculate iLIS between chains and generate ChimeraX scripts.

e.g., Fiber protein 2, pp71

AlphaFold DB Monomer Subdomain

Fetch monomer predictions from AlphaFold DB. Auto-detect domains and calculate intramolecular iLIS between domains.

e.g., Q8TF76 (Haspin), Q15858 (SCN9A)
